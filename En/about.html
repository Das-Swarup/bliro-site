About Us

Customer Success Management is tough role. You have to keep on top of everything, follow-ups and make sure everybody is happy.

In addition, CSMs are expected to complete manual administrative work such as logging information in Salesforce, gainsight, report to other departments via Slack, E-mail, morse code or smoke signals.

CSMs hold valuable and important information. How to make sure that the valuable information was logged and shared? How can we make this burdon less time consuming? How to make sure this data does not get lost?
