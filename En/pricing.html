Frequently asked questions

I don’t have admin rights on my laptop. What should I do?

Please reach out to us via hello@bliro.io. We will get back to and identify a way for you to install bliro.



Which tools do you integrate into?

We currently offer integrations into Salesforce, Gainsight (if connected to your Salesforce System), Slack, Emails and Microsoft Teams. We retrieve data from Google and Outlook Calender and Salesforce.

I need to integrate into a tool, that is currently not supported by bliro.

Please send en email to hello@bliro.io and describe which tool you need to integrate into. We will add it do our product roadmap and will notify you, once your desired integration is available.


Do I have control over which information is shared?

Yes! Nothing is documented and shared without you explicitly sending it to your colleagues or tools. You are always in control.

Is bliro really free to start?

Yes! We want you to experience bliro’s value yourself. Just download the free version and get going.


Can I talk to someone? I still have questions.

Yes, we would love to hear from you! Email us at hello@bliro.io.
